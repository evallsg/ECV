About Eligere

Eligere provides a space to create "choose your own adventure"-like stories together. You and your friends, or think-alike people from around the world, can collaborate to create infinite stories with hundreds of paths. Unleash the hive mind creativity.

Original

You choose how to continue, you choose your own adventure. From the same starting point, you can unravel a whole different story based on your decisions.

Collaborative

Its unique concept encourages collaboration to boost creativity. Create a story and let others collaborate in it, or add a new chapter to someone else's story.

Stylish Design

Visualize the book structure with a tree view that allows the user to see the paths, who is the author, if they are finished or if they are open for you to write them.

Fully Responsive

Eligiere has been designed with smartphones in mind, without neglecting the desktop view. Either you are at home or commuting, you will always be able to create your stories, or read and collaborate with others'.

Easy To Use

Simple application that allows the user to navigate throw the books effortlessly.

Free of Charge

Eligere is and will always be free.

How does Eligere works?

Sign-Up

Register in our system to get a user and start creating. It's free!

Read

Read all the books (finished and unfinished) that other users have written. You can also comment on them and make suggestions.

Create

Create your own book! You can define the title, the genre and the main structure of the story. You can write the chapters of your story or leave them to the users' creativity.

Collaborate

Collaborate in other users' book! You can leave comments and create new decisions and chapters.
